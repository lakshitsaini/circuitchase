Here are the rules

Circuit chase is an individual game, to be played on our own website. This website has been developed by the members of our club so that you get a personalized experience of the event!

Begin

The circuit chase starts with a clue given to you about a particular component used to make circuits.

Crack

Once you crack the clue and find the answer, you submit it on our website and move forward for another clue. If your answer is wrong, you can't move on to the next clue.

Solve

This way, you will get 5 clues for 5 circuit components. Once you guess all 5, you are given a 6th clue which tells you about how to assemble the circuit and prepare the final solution.

Submit

After solving all 6 clues and making the circuit, you have to send your final solution to us. The first one to send the circuit wins the chase!
